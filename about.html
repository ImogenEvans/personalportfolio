Who I am

I am a creator based in Middle England and have worked on a professional basis both in-house and remotely. I am a capable and motivated individual who is passionate about being the best designer I can be on each project I work on.

The arts play an important role in all aspects of life, from marketing to personal pleasure, which is why I'm searching for a job that will help me build upon these skills I already have. Maybe even a role that will help me to learn brand new skills if the opportunity presents itself.
